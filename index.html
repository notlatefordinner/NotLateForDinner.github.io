Manhattan

  • Time: 3 mins
  • Serves: one
Ingredients Quantity
Rye Whiskey 2 oz
Sweet Vermouth 1 oz
Angostura bitters 2 dashes
maraschino cherry 1

Step 1: Mix all ingredients in shaker with ice.

Step 2: Pour in a coupe, garnish.

Source: Liquor.com

Paris When It Sizzles

  • Time: 3 mins
  • Serves: one
Ingredients Quantity
Dark Rum 2 oz
Elderflower liqueur 3/4 oz
Angostura bitters 1 dashe
Fresh Lime juice 1/2 oz
Lime wedge 1

Step 1: Mix all ingredients in shaker with ice.

Step 2: Pour in a coupe, garnish.

Oaxaca Old-Fashioned

  • Time: 3 mins
  • Serves: one
Ingredients Quantity
Tequila 1.5 oz
Mezcal 1/2 oz
Angostura bitters 2 dashes
Agave nectar 1 barspoon
flamed orange peel 1

Step 1: Mix all ingredients in shaker with ice.

Step 2: Pour in a coupe, garnish.
