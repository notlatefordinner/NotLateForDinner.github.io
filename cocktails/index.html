Manhattan

  • Time: 3 mins
  • Serves: one
Ingredients Quantity
Rye Whiskey 2 oz
Sweet Vermouth 1 oz
Angostura bitters 2 dashes
maraschino cherry 1

Step 1: Mix all ingredients in shaker with ice.

Step 2: Pour in a coupe, garnish.

Vieux Carre

>
  • Time: 3 mins
  • Serves: one
Ingredients Quantity
Rye Whiskey 1 oz
Cognac 1 oz
Sweet Vermouth 1 oz
Benedictine 1 tsp
Peychaud's bitters 2 dashes
lemon twist 1

Step 1: stir all ingredients in shaker with ice.

Step 2: Strain into a rocks glass with ice, garnish.
