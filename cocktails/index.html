Daiquiri

Ingredients Quantity
Light rum 1.7 oz
Lime juice .5 oz
Simple syrup .3 oz
lime wedge 1

Step 1: Mix all ingredients in shaker with ice.

Step 2: Pour in a coupe, garnish..

Sazerac Cocktail

Ingredients Quantity
Rye Whiskey (try Canadian with orange bitters vs rye/brandy blend) 2.5 oz
absinthe 0.25 oz
vs Pernod plus 0.5 teaspoon
green Chartruese 1 teaspoon
simple syrup 1 teaspoon
Peychaud's bitters 3 dashes
lemon twist 1

Step 1: Chill glass, rinse with absinthe

Step 2: Pour in above mixture. Express twist oils onto drink.
